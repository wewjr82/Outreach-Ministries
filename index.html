Introduction of the Founder

Pastor Jenai D. Jackson

Prophetess Jenai is the daughter of Deacon David C. Jackson and Deaconess Norma J. Jackson and proud sister to her late Brother, Kamren M. Jackson. At the young age of 9 years old Prophetess Jenai Jackson received the Lord as her personal Savior. One night at a very young age, Evangelist Willie Riddick prophesied that Jenai would one day be a prophetess and preach the Gospel. Prophetess Jenai taught her first Bible lesson at the age of 16 at Holy Temple United Holy Church of America, Incorporated. At the age of 22 she preached her first sermon entitled, “Are You Connected to the Source.” She also went on to teach Sunday School, Vacation Bible School, Bible Study, and Children’s Church.

Prophetess Jenai is a native of Suffolk, Virginia where she graduated with honors from Nansemond River High School. After graduating high school, she went on to further her education at Saint Augustine’s University, a historically black college in Raleigh, North Carolina. In 2010, she started her first prayer group on campus with a close friend, Lady Shamyrah Davis. In 2012, Prophetess Jenai obeyed the voice of the Lord telling her to move back home to Virginia. She transferred to Old Dominion University where she later received her Bachelors of Science degree in Biology PreMed with a minor in Religion.

In 2016, Prophetess Jenai went on to become a Professional Personal Care Assistant, Medication Aide Technician, and a Phlebotomist. In 2017, Prophetess Jenai attended the Sentara College of Allied Health to receive her certificate as a Nursing Care Partner. In 2018, she began working as a Nursing Care Partner for Sentara Norfolk General Hospital. She later became a Certified Nursing Assistant and a Registered Medication Aide for the State of Virginia in 2019. Prophetess Jenai has won many awards while serving in the Level 1 Trauma Center at Sentara Norfolk General Hospital. In 2024 she began working as a Community Health Worker for Sentara Norfolk General Hospital. Prophetess Jenai uses every moment as a ministry moment.

By way of the Holy Spirit, Prophetess Jenai birthed her ministry, Destined for Greatness, Design with a Purpose Outreach Ministries in 2017. The mission of Destined for Greatness is to teach others how to pray, have mountain moving faith, show unconditional love, and spread God’s Holy Word through evangelism. Prophetess Jenai launched another ministry in 2020 during the global pandemic. God allowed Jenai, along with her family, to have a series of Friday Night Hour of Power Services. The Lord showed up and showed out by moving in a mighty way. The Jackson Family was able to be a blessing to others. In February 2022, Prophetess Jenai decided to further her education by attending Walden University where she will pick back up studying for her Masters in Clinical Mental Health Counseling in the near future.

Prophetess Jenai was elevated on September 17, 2022 to an ordained Elder and installed Pastor over her ministry Destined for Greatness, Designed with a Purpose Outreach Ministries by the Chief Apostle Mario L. Davis of Victory Christian International Fellowship of Churches, Incorporated. Pastor Jenai was appointed on September 15, 2024 as the first Supervisor of Women for the newest jurisdiction “Tidewater Ecclesiastical Jurisdiction” of Victory Christian International Fellowship of Churches, Incorporated by the Chief Apostle Mario L. Davis and the Presiding Prelate of the Tidewater Jurisdiction, Bishop Natasha Johnson. Pastor Jenai has served as the Youth Pastor for Freedom Worship Center, Incorporated. Pastor Jenai’s number one goal is to serve the Lord with gladness and build God’s kingdom so that lives can be changed.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to creating a welcoming, Safe Space for those who may not yet be part of the Body of Christ. Our mission is to Nurture a community where everyone feels accepted and valued, regardless of their background or current spiritual journey. Through compassionate guidance and teaching, we aim to inspire individuals to live lives marked by holiness and righteousness. We are committed to sharing the Transformative power of God's Word, both within our community and beyond, in all seasons of life.

Our Vision

Our vision is to lead souls to a life-changing encounter with Christ. We believe in the miraculous power of Deliverance, Healing, and the manifestation of Signs and Wonders that demonstrate God's active presence in our lives. Through these divine experiences, we seek to deepen Faith, restore Hope, and Empower individuals to walk in the freedom and abundant life that Christ offers.

Our Purpose

In a time when many in the church may be questioning their faith or feeling disconnected from an authentic, holy lifestyle, our purpose is clear. We are here to Affirm the undeniable reality of God's existence and His desire for a deep, personal relationship with each of us. We are committed to teaching the church, both Locally and Globally, that there is a practical, joy-filled way to live a Holy life that Honors God and Transforms the world around us.
